Lebron's Philanthropy

Lebron donated $2.5 million to Muhammad Ali exhibit

He created the Lebron James Family Foundation which established many other institutes like "I Promise". "I Promise" is a program that helps LJFF students through graduation. He also plans to have some more upcoming projects in NYC. LJFF also hosts a "We Are Family Reunion" annually welcoming a new group of families into the Wheels for Education Program. There is a partnership as well, which allows LJFF to highlight a public school student and his/her aspirations each week called "23 Big dreams".
